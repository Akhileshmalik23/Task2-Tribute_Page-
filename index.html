Major Glories

Dandi March

During the Salt March, Gandhi led thousands in a peaceful protest against the British salt tax. They walked 240 miles to the coast, symbolically making salt, igniting a nationwide civil disobedience movement.

Quit India Movement

The Quit India Movement was a mass civil disobedience campaign led by Gandhi, demanding an end to British rule in India. It mobilized millions, urging "Do or Die" for freedom. It challenged British authority, leading to widespread protests and arrests.
